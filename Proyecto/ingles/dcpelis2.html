CATALOG OF MOVIES AND SERIES OF DC COMICS.

DC Comics is an American publisher, part of DC Entertainment, which in turn it is part of the holding company of the entertainment monster Time Warner. Founded in 1934 under the name "National Allied Publications", changed in 1937 to DC Comics, short for Detective Comics, one of the first successes of the company DC Comics is recognized worldwide for its universe of superheroes, among which are Superman, Batman, Wonder Woman, Flash, Green Lantern, Aquaman, Green Arrow and groups of heroes such as the League of Justice or The Teen Titans. The publisher also publishes comics not related to its heroic universe, such as Watchmen, V for Vendetta or The Sandman. But these works, like many others, are published under his other labels, such as Vertigo or Milestone. Stocking.

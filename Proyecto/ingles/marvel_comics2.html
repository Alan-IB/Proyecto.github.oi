Worldwide, Inc., known as Marvel Comics, is an American comic book publisher created in 1939, initially under the name Timely Publications. Among its emblematic characters of the superheroic genre are Spider-Man, Wolverine, X-Men, Captain America, Iron Man, Hulk, Thor, The Fantastic 4, Daredevil, Punisher, The Avengers, among other. From the 1990s, the company was positioned as one of the main comic book publishers in the country. On August 31, 2009, The Walt Disney Company purchased Marvel Entertainment. for about $4 billion, merging with it on January 1, 2010.
